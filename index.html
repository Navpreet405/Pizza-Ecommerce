FAQ

How do I place an order?

Simply select your favorite pizzas, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can customize each pizza with your preferred toppings and crust options before finalizing your order. or yo can call at our number and place an order.

Is there a minimum order for delivery?

Yes, the minimum order amount for delivery is $15. For orders under this amount, we encourage in-store pickup to save on delivery fees and get your pizza fresh and hot.

can I order for large events?

Yes, we offer catering for large events like parties and corporate gatherings. You can place bulk orders in advance, and we’ll help customize the menu to suit your event’s needs. Just contact us for details!

What are your delivery hours?

Our delivery hours are from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM every day. Orders can be placed anytime within this window for same-day delivery.

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes! We offer gluten-free crusts and a variety of vegan toppings, so you can enjoy a delicious pizza that meets your dietary needs without compromising on flavor
